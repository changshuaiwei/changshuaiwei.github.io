Welcome to Changshuai Wei's Home Page

Epidemiology and Biostatistics in Human Medcine

I'm Dr. Changshuai Wei, assistant professor in the Department of Biostatistics and Epidemiology at UNTHSC. My research expertise are statistical genetics, non-parametric methods and machine learning. My primary research interest is to develop novel statistical methods to investigate genetic etiology of human complex diseases by using high-dimensional human genetics data. I have developed tree-based and U-statistic methods and implemented my methods to computationally efficient software to investigate the complex interactions and heterogeneity of human diseases. I'm also interested in application of different machine learning methods and risk prediction research. I have built risk prediction models for Crohn's Disease and Nicotine dependence using massively large Genome-wide Association data and Sequencing data. Besides my independent research, I always enjoyed collaborating with other investigators in a wide range of biomedical and public health research.
